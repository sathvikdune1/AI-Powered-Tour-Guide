Description

The Maldives is an archipelago nation in the Indian Ocean, renowned for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and luxurious resorts. Composed of over 1,190 coral islands grouped into 26 atolls, the Maldives is a tropical paradise like no other.

The Maldives is synonymous with luxury and relaxation. Overwater bungalows, private villas, and world-class spas are the hallmarks of the Maldivian resort experience.

DAY 01
Malé

While often overlooked for the island resorts, Malé offers a unique experience. As the capital, it provides a glimpse into the local Maldivian life. Explore the bustling fish market, visit the historic Hukuru Miskiiy Mosque, and soak up the vibrant atmosphere.

DAY 02
North Malé Atoll

Home to some of the most luxurious resorts in the world, the North Malé Atoll is the epitome of Maldivian luxury. Indulge in world-class spa treatments, savor exquisite cuisine, and immerse yourself in the breathtaking underwater world.

DAY 03
South Malé Atoll

Offering a mix of luxury resorts and affordable guesthouses, the South Malé Atoll caters to diverse travelers. Enjoy water sports, explore vibrant coral reefs, or simply relax on pristine beaches.
