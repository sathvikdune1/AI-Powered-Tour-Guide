Description

Tourism in America is a vibrant and diverse industry, attracting millions of visitors each year to its vast array of attractions and destinations. From the bustling cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago, known for their cultural landmarks, entertainment, and dining.

The historic sites in Washington, D.C., the sandy beaches of Florida and California, and the unique charm of places like New Orleans and San Francisco further enhance its appeal.

DAY 01
National Mall

While the Washington Monument towers above all else—quite literally, the city doesn’t zone for buildings taller than the marble icon

DAY 02
Niagara Falls

The three waterfalls (Horseshoe Falls, American Falls and Bridalveil Falls) that make up Niagra Falls have the combined highest flow rate of any waterfall worldwide.

DAY 03
Statue Of Liberty

The 151-foot-tall copper monument is worth seeing up close from aboard the free Staten Island Ferry, but you can also pay your respects in person and even step inside it while on an official tour.
