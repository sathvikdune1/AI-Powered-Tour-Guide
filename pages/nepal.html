Description

Nepal is a landlocked country nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, bordered by China to the north and India to the south, east, and west. Known for its breathtaking mountain landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, Nepal is a dream destination for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and cultural enthusiasts alike.

Tourism is a major industry in Nepal, drawing visitors from around the world to experience its natural beauty and cultural richness.

DAY 01
Kathmandu

As Nepal's capital, Kathmandu is a bustling metropolis with a rich cultural heritage. It's a perfect blend of ancient temples, vibrant markets, and modern city life.

DAY 02
Pokhara

Nestled amidst the Annapurna Himalayas, Pokhara is a nature lover's paradise. Known for its stunning lakes, mountains, and adventure activities, it's a perfect escape.

DAY 03
Lumbini

A sacred pilgrimage site for Buddhists worldwide, Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha. It offers a serene atmosphere and a deep spiritual connection.
