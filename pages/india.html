Description

India is a vast and culturally rich country in South Asia. Known as the world's largest democracy, it is home to over 1.4 billion people, making it the most populous nation globally.

India has emerged as a global economic powerhouse, with a rapidly growing IT sector and a burgeoning middle class. Agriculture remains the backbone of the economy, employing a significant portion of the population.

DAY 01
Taj Mahal

No trip to India is complete without witnessing the ethereal beauty of the Taj Mahal. This iconic white marble mausoleum is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of eternal love.

DAY 02
Goa

Goa is India's sunshine state, offering pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, delicious seafood, and a laid-back atmosphere. Perfect for relaxation, adventure, or cultural exploration.

DAY 03
Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala boasts lush greenery, serene backwaters, beautiful beaches, and Ayurvedic wellness retreats. It's an ideal destination for nature lovers and those seeking rejuvenation.
